I am Asja Bajramović

A ballerina and a programmer

Hi everyone, my name is Asja Bajramović.

As I already said, I am a strange creature when it comes to doing what I love. Just like I found two of the things I love the most - programming and ballet - people said they're incompatible. But I found a way to break the social boundaries and show the world ballerinas are not just small, weak girls in tutus , that they can do everything they set their mind to.
I have a lot of goals I have set my mind to. I'm planning on finishing an IT college and working an IT job. Aside from that, I am creative and love writing and drawing, all self-thaught. I wish to draw and sell the drawings as another job.
Dancing ballet for over ten years, I fell deeply in love with it. It went on that far, that I had a class of around fifteen little girls (age 3-6) to teach in my ballet club, at the age of seventeen. That's also a job I could potentially do.
Dancing in a lot of ballet plays and shows and having to, of course, act in them, I had a lot of actors and teachers tell me my acting skills are exquisite. Even though I have thought about taking acting classes as a kid, that surely made me want to act more. Perhaps in the future, I'd succeed as an actress. I'd surely like that.
